FAQ's

1. What do I need to bring to a yoga class?

All you need to bring is comfortable clothing that allows you to move freely, your personal mats, towels and a water bottle.

2. Is it necessary to register for classes in advance?

While it's not necessary to register in advace. While we recommend doing so to ensure your spot in a class.

3. Do I need to be flexible to do yoga ?

No, you don't need to be flexible to do yoga. These practices are designed to help improve flexibility and balance.
